The Evolution of the Macbook

    The Macbook was produced by the company called Apple. The main difference between the apple computer and other computers is that Macbooks have high-quality graphic performance, svelte design and the MacOS. Apple had announced the first MacBook Pro on January 10,2006. It had weighed 5.6 pounds with a 1-inch thickness and boasted a 15-inch screen. It delievered four times the performance of the PowerBook G4.
